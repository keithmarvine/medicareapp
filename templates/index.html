What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring a valid photo ID, your insurance card, a list of current medications, any relevant medical records from previous providers, and completed patient intake forms if you've downloaded them from our website. Arriving 15 minutes early helps ensure a smooth check-in process.

Do you accept my insurance?

We accept most major insurance plans including Medicare and Medicaid. Our billing department can verify your coverage before your appointment. If you have questions about your specific plan, please call our office and our staff will be happy to assist you in determining your benefits and coverage.

What are your office hours?

We are open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and major holidays. For after-hours emergencies, please call our main number and follow the prompts to reach our on-call physician.

How long will my appointment take?

Initial consultations typically last 45-60 minutes, while follow-up appointments are usually 20-30 minutes. Specific procedures or diagnostic tests may require additional time. We'll inform you of the expected duration when you schedule your appointment.

Can I get my test results online?

Yes! We offer a secure patient portal where you can access test results, medical records, appointment history, and communicate with your healthcare team. You'll receive login credentials after your first visit.
